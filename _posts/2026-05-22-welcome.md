We are excited to officially launch the website for **TANG LAB @ WHU**!

This platform will serve as a hub to share our latest research projects, publication highlights, developed software tools, teaching resources, and exciting joining opportunities.

Our laboratory is dedicated to pioneering **Physics-Intrinsic Artificial Intelligence (PI-AI)** to advance circular resource and sustainable engineering science. We leverage machine learning-accelerated molecular dynamics (MLIP-AIMD), generative deep learning, and advanced materials design to address cross-disciplinary frontiers and critical challenges at the intersections of **energy, environment, resources, agriculture, and chemical engineering**.

We warmly invite you to explore our pages to learn more about our team and current research. Please stay tuned for our upcoming updates, news, and breakthroughs! If you are interested in collaborating or joining our group as a researcher or graduate student, please do not hesitate to [contact us](mailto:siqit@outlook.com).
