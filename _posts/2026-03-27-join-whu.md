title: "Dr. Tang Joins Wuhan University as Research Associate Professor"
---
On March 27, 2026, Dr. Siqi Tang officially joined the School of Resource and Environmental Sciences at Wuhan University as a research-tracked Associate Professor.

Dr. Tang received his Ph.D. in Mechanics (Energy and Resources Engineering) and M.S. in Environmental Engineering from Peking University, and his B.S. in Environmental Engineering from Northwest Normal University. Prior to joining Wuhan University, he built a strong research background through postdoctoral and researcher appointments at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), Peking University, and Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

At Wuhan University, Dr. Tang leads the **TANG LAB @ WHU**, which focuses on **Physics-Intrinsic Artificial Intelligence (PI-AI)** to advance circular resource and sustainable engineering science. The lab leverages machine learning-accelerated molecular dynamics (MLIP-AIMD), generative deep learning, and advanced materials design to tackle critical challenges in molecular systems, sustainable materials, and circular resource technologies.

We warmly welcome Dr. Tang to the school and look forward to the pioneering scientific contributions and discoveries from the TANG LAB under his leadership!
