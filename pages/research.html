Theme 1

Physics-intrinsic AI (PI-AI) for Sustainable Materials and CO₂ Mineralization

TANG LAB studies how Physics-intrinsic Artificial Intelligence (PI-AI), molecular simulation, and data-driven workflows can accelerate the discovery and optimization of materials for carbon mineralization and environmental remediation. A key direction connects AIMD and machine-learning workflows to investigate artificially accelerated mineralization of CO₂ on hydroxyapatite material families.

From Atoms to Systems

TANG LAB connects atomistic simulation, machine-learning models, and system-level sustainability assessment. This "atoms-to-systems" perspective allows the group to study not only how materials and molecular processes behave, but also how emerging technologies may perform environmentally, economically, and socially.
